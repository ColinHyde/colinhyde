Quick and Easy Bean Burrito

An easy and hearty bean burrito!

Ingredients

  • Rosarita canned refried beans
  • Large flour tortillas

Directions

  1. Lay out 1 large flour tortilla on a flat working surface
  2. Open can of refriend beans (recommended opening from the bottom of the can as they will come out easier)
  3. Place tube of beans on tortilla
  4. Wrap tortilla around beans in your prefered wrapping method
  5. Enjoy!
